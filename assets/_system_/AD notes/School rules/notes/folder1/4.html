USE OF MOBILE PHONES
The use of mobile phones is strictly forbidden for Middle School students on school grounds.
Such phones as are in their possession must be invisible (in a binder or locker).
Only High School students are allowed to use them in the Student Lounge. The rest of the time, phones must be off and put
away. Non respect will result in confiscation by the Vie Scolaire (See section 7: The Vie Scolaire).
The use of mobile phones in class is also strictly prohibited even for educational purposes. Tablets for language study as
well as the computers available in the school make the pedagogic use of mobile phones redundant.
