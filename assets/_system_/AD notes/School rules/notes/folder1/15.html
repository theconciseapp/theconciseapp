CONNECTIVITY (TELEPHONES, TABLETS, ETC.)

Telephone calls: they are prohibited in the school grounds in any form whatsoever (phone, sms, internet).
Watching videos is allowed in the Student Lounge provided that the content remains appropriate.
In class, electronic devices must be turned off and stored unless specifically requested by the teacher. The same applies to all personal items brought into the establishment that do not have a direct relationship with school
activities.
In case of non-compliance with these rules, such devices will be confiscated and returned to their owner during an
appointment with the C.P.E. for the first offense or the Principal for a second offense.
The taking of photographs and videos on school grounds is strictly forbidden, unless authorized by the Principal.
The use of the internet and digital material is under the responsibility of the teacher and only for educational purposes in
accordance with the rules of the computer charter.
