HYGIENE, HEALTH AND SAFETY
11.1. HYGIENE
The school premises are kept clean. Drinking water and retractable silicone cups (sold at the Vie Scolaire Office for 25,000
Kips) are available to students. It is the responsibility of everyone to maintain all school facilities in good condition and to
ensure respect for the environment.

11.2. HEALTH
In case of sickness, discomfort or accident occurring during school hours, the student will be taken to the secretariat, or the
infirmary which has a bed and first aid equipment. The family and health services will be immediately notified.
If a student is undergoing treatment that requires medication, the family should inform the nurse and the school service in
writing. The student will deposit the drugs at school and will follow the dosage with the help of the head of the Vie Scolaire.
A medical visit for students entering 6th and 2nd Form may be offered if a nurse is present. The introduction, sale and
incitement to use alcohol, tobacco and illegal substances of any kind are strictly prohibited.

11.3. SAFETY

11.3.1. Persons
Safety instructions are posted in each class room. An evacuation or lock-down exercise is organized once every quarter.
11.3.2. Insurance

The school has insurance covering all activities organized by the school in Laos and neighboring countries. In the case of a
school trip to a non-neighboring country, it is possible to take out supplementary cover with the same insurer for the sum
of 10 USD.

11.3.3. Responsibility
During the school hours as defined by the school timetable, students are under the full responsibility of the teachers. Roll
call is made in each class within 15 minutes of the beginning of the lesson, in order to better manage absences. Travel
outside classrooms must be exceptional.
Outside school hours, the Vie Scolaire ensures that student behavior and activities do not pose a risk to individuals or
locals.

11.3.4. Motorized students

Wearing a helmet is mandatory. Students seen coming without one will be given written notification, and not be allowed
to use the school parking lot.

11.3.5. Property

A shelter for bikes and a motorcycle is available to students. However, the school cannot be held responsible in case of theft.
