App Builder is an app that makes other apps. Android apps are usually designed on a desktop computer and written in a programming language, but there is an easier way to do it -- by using App Builder. In App Builder, you design your app mostly by editing the app tree - a hierarchy of nodes. Other pieces of your app, such as HTML content and images, may be stored in App Files.
